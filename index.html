HSI Spectral Analysis

Real-time Wavelength Decomposition

Our proprietary AI algorithms break down hyperspectral imaging data across wide wavelength bands (400nm - 1000nm+), allowing the system to peer beneath the fruit's surface.

  • Peak Recognition: Identifies distinct spectral signatures for diseases and sugar concentration.
  • Non-Invasive: 100% safe inspection without physical penetration or chemical marking.
  • Micro-Defect Detection: Finds internal necrosis and bruising 3-5 days before they are visible to the naked eye.

Deep Structural Analysis (Advanced HSI)

Automatically detects hidden bruises, fungal diseases, assesses ripeness, measures sweetness (Brix), and screens for pesticide residues.
